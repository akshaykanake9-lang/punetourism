About Us

I am Akshay Kananke, and along with my partners Parth, Adhiraaj, Dipanshu, Vaibhav, and Siddharth, we created this website as a micro project. This website is specially designed to guide people who are not from Pune and may find it difficult to explore the city. Pune has many historical places, food spots, and important locations, but new visitors often lack proper information about routes, timings, and facilities. Our aim is to provide simple and useful guidance so that tourists can easily travel, save time, and enjoy the city without confusion.

PUNE "Oxford of the East"

Welcome to Pune, a vibrant city blending history and modern life. Once the stronghold of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Pune still reflects its rich heritage through forts and traditions. Today, it is a major educational and IT hub, often called the Oxford of the East. The city is known for its pleasant climate, youthful vibe, and growing infrastructure. Pune is most famous for its colleges, festivals, and food like misal pav.


If you are new to Pune, this website is a tour guide for you. We will guide you through various places to visit and provide info like whether it is free or not, timings, and transport like PMPML buses. Also, best places to eat and explore.
